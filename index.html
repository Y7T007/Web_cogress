About The Workshop

Save the date : 15 and 16 November 2023

  • 1

    The need for Mathematical models, digital, physical, or logistical solutions is steadily increasing in medicine. These tools formally represent complex phenomena, facilitating enhanced understanding and improved therapeutic approaches. Intelligent solutions play a central role in the future of medicine, facilitating advancements in assisted surgery, detecting tumors, remote patient monitoring, and personalized treatments. Furthermore, intelligent solutions encompass smart medical and healthcare devices, which have become increasingly prevalent in clinical settings. These devices include sensors, insulin pumps, inhalers, respiratory ventilators, implants, microcapsules, and nanoparticles for targeted drug delivery.


    Mathematical Models: Driving Medicine's Future

  • 2

    Despite the impressive clinical results achieved through these novel technologies, various scientific and technological challenges persist. Meeting these challenges requires multidisciplinary approaches and expertise, combining mathematics, computation, medicine, chemistry, and physics knowledge. Bridging clinical experience with academic research is crucial in addressing these obstacles.


    Intelligent Solutions: Transforming Healthcare Challenges

  • 3

    This workshop aims to foster innovation and improve decision-support tools in the healthcare sector. By bringing together researchers from diverse complementary disciplines, the workshop aims to facilitate collaboration and the exchange of ideas, enabling scientists to present their latest research findings. This international and multidisciplinary event is a forum to advance the development of solutions that benefit the healthcare industry.


    Innovation Workshop: Advancing Healthcare Decision-Support

  • 4

    We invite you to share your research and insights at the international workshop on Mathematical modeling, intelligent solutions, devices, and systems for medical decision support 2023 (WMDS23).


    WMDS23: Advancements in Medical Solutions
